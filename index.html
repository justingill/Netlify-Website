About Me

Welcome to my website!
My name is Justin Gill and I am a data analyst/scientist. I have conducted many different analysis and models ranging from time-series to unstructured data scraped from websites. My education has given me the tools to engage myself in the field of data science, while also being able to progressively learn new data analysis tools. I have taken multiple courses outside of my education to further myself and have dived into classic books in data science such as Python for Data Analysis and Intro to Statistical Learning. My last internship at the Desert Research Institute in Reno, Nevada allowed me to work with crop growers in Gilroy, California and Yerington, Nevada. I successfully forecasted soil moisture for many different crop fields to allow the farmers a better opportunity to have data driven decisions in their respective fields. I have sinced moved to Fremont, California and am currently looking to apply my current skills in a variety of different industries so I can help organizations make data-driven decisions and grow myself further.
